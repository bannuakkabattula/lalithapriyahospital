WHY WE ARE

Arogya Sri: Affordable Healthcare for All

At Lalitha Priya Hospital, we are committed to making healthcare accessible to everyone. We understand that quality medical care should never be out of reach, and that's why we are proud to offer the Arogya Sri program to our patients.

  • Quality Care:- Rest assured that you will receive the same high-quality medical care that all our patients enjoy. Arogya Sri doesn't compromise on quality, and neither do we.
  • Affordability - Arogya Sri significantly reduces the financial strain of medical treatments. You can focus on your health without worrying about the cost.
  • Extensive Coverage -he program covers a wide array of treatments and procedures, making it a comprehensive option for your healthcare needs.
  • Eligibility: - Arogya Sri is designed to benefit families with limited financial resources. To determine your eligibility and understand how this program can help you, our dedicated team is here to guide you through the process.
